Short presentation

I am a PhD student in Télécom Paris. My PhD is co-supervised by Ada Diaconescu and Jean-Louis Dessalles. My research interests lie in machine learning, complex adaptive systems, explanability and causality.

My PhD

I currently work on explainability techniques for complex open cyber-physical systems. This includes methods to generate causal explanations, to identify actual causes, or to produce relevant explanations. You can find a summary here

Research interests

While my PhD mainly focuses on explainability, I am interested in most subjects that relates to artificial intelligence, complex adaptive systems, and causality. I previously worked on deep learning for natural language processing and knowledge graphs. I also have a strong interest for the societal impacts of modern AI such as LLMs or recommendation systems.
